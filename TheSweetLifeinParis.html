The Sweet Life in Paris
The Sweet Life in Paris is a 2016 American comedy-drama film starring Chris Messina and Abigail Spencer.

Plot

Kenny Parker and Lolita Nowicki, each struggling with their own brand of demons, meet by chance in Chicago and form a pact to travel across the country to the Golden Gate Bridge to commit suicide together.


Cast
  • Chris Messina as Kenny Pantalio.
  • Abigail Spencer as Lolita Nowicki.
  • Maggie Siff as Ava.
  • Tyson Ritter as Marlon.
  • J. D. Evermore as Brandon.
  • Jayne Brook as Katherine.
  • Karan Soni as Convenience Store Clerk.
  • Nick Searcy as County Sheriff.
